THE ULTIMATE DIRESKIES GUIDE

Introduction

DireSkies is a Minecraft Server hosted on the Server Network MineHut.
The Server Owner is Seth (InGameName: 6l8, Aqmin or Direskies).
The Server DireSkies was first realesed on December 16th 2022 at 3PM CST.
DireSkies is a Skymining Server, that means you are playing on island in the sky with different mines on it.
If you mine the blocks from the mines you will get the material the mine is made of.
Each Mine is a 9*9*9 block cube, exept the Redstone and Lapis lazuli mines in the Mainland and the Glowstone mines in the Nether.

With the materials you obtained from mining you can upgrade your pickaxe, armor and sword.
